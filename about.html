About Us

We are Untouchables Barbershop. We definetely aren't your typical Barbershop. Our team members are fully liscenced and we all come from very diverse backgrounds. The vibe of our shop will have you wanting to come in on a weekly basis. Cut your hair with us once and you won't want to leave the shop! Tapers, Mid-Fades, Beard Lineups , we do it all.

What We Stand For

Quality Haircuts is what we strive for. We want all of our clients to trust us with their hair because it can either make or break your week. No more red rashes on your scalp from dirty clippers. We make sure to sanitize our tools after every cut and at the end of the night so you know that you are getting the freshest of cuts.

Roger Williams (Owner)

Roger Williams is the CEO of Untouchables Barbershop. He to started his journey when he was a teenager. Roger didn't come from the best background however that never stopped him from pursuing and accomplishing his dream. He understands the hardships that life brings and how one faulty decision that is commited can effect the rest of your life. Or how because of where you grew up or your race many doors are shut. Roger wished to give people a second chance which is why he opened his shop, he fulfilled his dream and wants to help others do the same. Now 30 years of age, Roger is succesfully living his dream and helping others do the same while providing the community with quality haircuts.
