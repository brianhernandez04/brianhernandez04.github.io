Our Team of Talented Barbers

Each one of our barbers are fully liscensed and ready to bless you with a haircut. We focus on the details of a fade to provide only the best possible haircut.

Kevin Owens

Kevin Owens started cutting hair since 2009. He moved to Anaheim,CA after hurrican Katrina to get a fresh new start to a life that he felt he was in control of. Through hard work and perseverance he managed to get his barbers liscence and now resides here at Untouchables Barbershop.

Angel Gutierrez

Angel Gutierrez was an ex convict. He started cutting hair as soon as he got out. He describes barbering as his way out and a way for him to show that he is truly good at something. He recieved his liscence and became a liscenced barber working as a apart of our team.

Stacy Lee

Stacy Lee came to the U.S as an immigrant and always struggled to make ends meet here. At first she would regret even coming here due to the struggles but became friends with a fellow barber and the rest is history. Once she got her liscense she was on board with the team.

Dean White

Dean White grew up bouncing from home to home. He started off cutting his friends hair and began to enjoy it. Along the way he discovered that he would want to be doing this for the rest of his life. It's his passion. To fully be able to enjoy his passion he attained his liscence and now works with us.
